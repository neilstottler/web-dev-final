BUDGETING

Members: Roberto, Neil, Miranda and Gurkaran

Budgeting is an essential practice when learning to save money. Although we have found that there are many ways to budget. We have compiled different examples of budgeting techniques for the user to become familiar with the concept of budgeting.

5 Core Reasons Why Budgeting Is Important

  1. Allows you to monitor your spending.
  2. Increases personal ability to save money for expenses or liesure.
  3. Budgeting can allow you to develop better spending habits.
  4. If there is a time where you need to spend more than predicted, budgeting can help save money for that time.
  5. Budgeting can help during tax season when determing how much is owed and how to spend money before tax returns.

HOW TO BUDGET

  1. Calculate your income, choose how you wish to budget your spending and monitor the progress.
  2. Allow up to 50% of your income for needs.
  3. Leave 20-30% of your income for wants.
  4. Determine possible payments (creditcard, taxes, ect).
  5. Sort through reciepts to determine weekly or monthly spendings to get an idea of annual spending.
